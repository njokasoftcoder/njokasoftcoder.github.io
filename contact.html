Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and engagement process

What sectors do you serve?

We specialize in NGOs, Healthcare, Research institutions, Government agencies, and Private sector organizations across East Africa.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we provide a free 30-minute initial consultation to understand your needs and discuss potential solutions.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary by project scope. Proposal writing: 2-4 weeks, EMR implementation: 4-8 weeks, Research studies: 8-12 weeks.

Do you provide training materials?

Yes, all training programs include comprehensive manuals, reference materials, and post-training support resources.

Can you work remotely?

Yes, we offer remote consulting and training services, with periodic on-site visits as needed for implementations.

What EMR systems do you support?

We specialize in OpenMRS, DHIS2, KenyaEMR, IQCare, and can customize solutions based on client needs.
